Commerce Performance Dashboard

Superset + SQL + KPI Design

üß© Business Case Summary

Commercial data was spread across multiple reports, making it difficult for managers to see the full picture. My goal was to bring revenue, costs, margin, inventory, and losses into one interactive solution, with consistent plan vs fact tracking, year-over-year comparison, and execution metrics. The result is a clear and reliable view of performance that supports faster and more confident decisions.

üìä What I built

I developed a multi-tab Superset dashboard covering Revenue, Purchasing Costs, Trade Margin, Markup, Inventory Turnover, Losses, Write-offs, and Bonuses. Each tab follows the same structure, with KPI cards showing current results, YoY difference, plan variance, and execution %. The Revenue module (shown in screenshots) also includes trend charts for plan execution and a drill-down table by format, region, store, supplier, and SKU hierarchy. I paid special attention to design, ensuring clean visuals, consistent color logic, and user-friendly layouts.

üí° Business Value

The dashboard replaced fragmented Excel reporting with a single, real-time source of truth. Managers now quickly see deviations, explore performance across any dimension, and identify root causes in just a few clicks. Strong visual design made adoption easy and turned the dashboard into a daily decision-making tool across departments.

  • End-to-end solution including SQL modeling, KPI logic, and Superset build
  • Consistent KPI cards with plan vs fact, year-over-year, and execution metrics
  • Interactive trend analysis with drill-down to region, store, supplier, and SKU
  • Professional design focused on clarity, usability, and adoption
